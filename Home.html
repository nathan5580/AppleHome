Welcome to the official information site for the Nathan Wilcké application. Here you'll find the privacy statement, data‑handling details, and terms of use required for App Store submission.

Privacy Policy

We take your data protection very seriously. Our application:

  • Collects no personal data without your explicit consent.
  • Does not require you to create an account.
  • Uses no third‑party advertising or tracking services.
  • Stores all data locally on your device and deletes it when you uninstall the app.

If you have questions about privacy, contact us at privacy@nathanwilcke.com.

Data Collection & Usage

In line with Apple's App Privacy Details, we confirm that the app:

  • Collects none of the data categories listed by Apple (e.g., location, contacts, identifiers).
  • Does not leverage any third parties for analytics.

This statement will be updated if app features evolve. Last updated on April 20, 2025.
